2003 Gold Cup & NA Video with Results

by Deb Whitehorse | Oct 17, 2016 | 2016, Home Page

From our friends in the New England Ice Yacht Association, a video look-back to the 2003 Gold Cup and North American championships.  Scorer Loretta Rehe searched her archives today and found the results. Identify away!

UPDATE: Click here to read to read Rosemary Hamill’s story about 2003 North American Championship, “A Champion’s Cheering Section – Our View from the Race Pits“.

From the NEIYA post: “From deep down in the bowels of the video vault, what is believed to be the only full video coverage of the 2003 DN Gold Cub and North Americans has surfaced.

We have added a few notes to the video. Can you find yourself or fellow competitors out there on the ice? If so leave a comment about where on the video and we will add a note for all to see. Any commentary is welcome. Jot em down now as you will forget later.”
